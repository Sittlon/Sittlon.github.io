Support for Poly Pew Pew

Find your installed version in the game settings or store listing. Describe the problem as precisely as possible and include this version.

Contact

Email robertsittig1996@web.de. Support is available in English and German.

Useful details include platform (Android/iOS), device, operating system, app version, the last actions before the problem and the exact text of any error. Include an anonymous player UID or support code only if the app displays it and it is necessary for the case.

Never send: passwords, Firebase tokens, complete card/bank details, identity-document copies or other particularly sensitive information. For purchase issues, a store order reference may be sufficient; attachments are not required.

Open support email

Your email application opens when you click. This website does not itself transmit form data. The privacy policy explains how support messages are handled.

Frequently asked questions

The game does not start or crashes.

Restart the device, update the operating system and Poly Pew Pew through the store, and check available storage. Tell support your device, OS/app version and when the crash occurred. Do not uninstall immediately: your local progress may be lost.

Touch controls, performance or audio do not work correctly.

Check the control, graphics and audio settings, close other resource-intensive apps and test after a restart. Tell support which setting and game mode are affected.

I cannot start an online match.

The online feature is in beta and needs a stable internet connection. Check that the game is current, try switching between Wi-Fi and mobile data, and reopen the lobby. Offline bot matches remain available without an online account.

My local progress is gone.

The complete save is generally stored on your device and is not currently fully synchronised to Firebase. The operator cannot remotely restore it after app-data deletion, uninstallation or a device change. Check whether your operating system offers a device backup.

My voluntary-ad reward is missing.

Wait briefly, check the internet connection and restart the app. Send support the app version, platform and approximate time. An advertising ID or screenshots of the privacy dialog are normally unnecessary.

A purchase failed or crystals are missing.

First check whether Apple App Store or Google Play completed the purchase, then restart the app. If the credit is missing, send only the store order reference—never complete payment details. Request refunds from the relevant store: Apple billing support or Google Play refunds.

How do I report an inappropriate display name?

Send the visible name, approximate time and any lobby/match context to support. Do not republish the name elsewhere. There is currently no free-form chat.

How do I change advertising and tracking choices?

Before advertising is enabled, the app must offer an easy-to-find privacy option in its settings. If it is missing from your test version, tell support; consent-dependent advertising must not then be active in production. On iOS, you can additionally manage tracking under Privacy & Security → Tracking; Android provides advertising-ID controls. The app must remain usable without personalised advertising or iOS tracking permission.

How do I delete local data or my online account?

Delete local data through the app's corresponding function, the operating system's app-data controls, or by uninstalling. Use the separate delete online account page for the anonymous Firebase identity and online data. Uninstalling alone does not delete server-side data.

How do I report a security vulnerability?

Email the support address with subject “Security Poly Pew Pew”. Describe impact and reproducible steps without accessing, altering or publicly disclosing other people's data. We will coordinate further handling privately.
